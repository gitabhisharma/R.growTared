Area Of Expertise.

R.Grow Tared Company has been a connoisseur of infrastructure development for the last 5 years! Our focus on excellence and precision has allowed us to revolutionise the construction industry.

We take pride in being one of the oldest and most efficient companies in the construction industry and have unlocked many achievements in the stellar journey of five years. From setting international records in roads and highway network projects to crafting civil administration buildings, R.grow tared has become a symbol of trust and innovation in the construction industry.

Our operation domains can be classified into two major fields: Construction contract business and the second is the development of roads and highway networks across the country. We offer EPC services for reputed clients specialising in the roads and highways sector, including long stretch highways, bridges and a vivid range of building and other civil construction projects. The second branch of our business concerning the development of roads and highway projects is currently working on the hybrid annuity model ("HAM") basis (the "Annuity Business"), and through our Subsidiaries completed build, operate, transfer ("BOT") annuity projects.
